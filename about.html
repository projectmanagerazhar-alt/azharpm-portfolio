Azharuddin Mohammed is a Senior Fit-Out Project Manager with 10+ years of UAE and KSA experience and a career portfolio of AED 110M+ spanning 46+ projects across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Jeddah, and Riyadh.

He is currently Project Manager — Fit-Out & Retail Expansion at Omorfia Group, leading the Bedashing Beauty UAE & KSA store rollout. To date this programme covers 14 concurrent projects (AED 30M+ approved CAPEX, 43,800+ sqft delivered) with AED 3.9M+ in CAPEX savings and AED 1.2M+ in contractor variation claims rejected — all contracts held at agreed value.

Previously, he spent seven years at StudioX Technical Works LLC / NStyle International as Fit-Out Project Manager — delivering 32+ projects across three disciplines: 18+ NStyle retail branch renovations and new openings, 5 F&B shell & core projects for Sapori di Bice and BiceMare in Dubai (AED 23M aggregate programme), and 8 high-end residential villa restorations.

Across both roles he has personally led authority submissions across DM, ADM, DCD/ADCD, DEWA, SEWA, DHA, plus mall management approvals for every project — building cross-discipline UAE regulatory expertise. He started his career as a Project Engineer (MEP) at Power Bright Electromechanical Works, providing the technical foundation that underpins his coordination work today.

He holds an Executive MBA in Project Management (BIMS College, 2023), a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering, and is an Autodesk AutoCAD Certified User. PMP is enrolled and in progress (2026).
